Steps to Embed & Encrypt Hidden Text Using LSB


Prepare Text for Embedding:


Step 1: Launch the Application
Click Embed Text to open the application.

Step 2: Select the Video File
Click on the "Browse" button to select the video file you want to use.
The application then calculates the total number of frames in the video and displays this information.

Step 3: Frame Selection
Enter the frame numbers where you want the text to be embedded. These should be specified as comma-separated values (e.g., 1,5,10).

Step 4: Enter the Text to Embed
Type the text you want to embed in the selected frames in the "Enter Embedded Text" field.

Step 5: Specify the Output Video File
Provide a path and filename for the output video that will contain the embedded text.

Step 6: Enter Encryption Key/Password
Type in a password or encryption key that will be used to encrypt the text.

Step 7: Choose Encryption Strength and Cipher Mode
Select the desired encryption strength (AES-128 or AES-256) and cipher mode (CBC - Cipher Block Chaining or GCM - Galois/Counter Mode) from the dropdown menus.

Step 8: Embed and Encrypt the Text
Click on the "Embed Text on Frames" button. The application will then:
1. Encrypt the text using the specified key, strength, and cipher mode.
2. Convert the encrypted text into a series of bits.
3. Apply error correction to these bits (using Reed-Solomon codes).
4. Embed these bits into the least significant bits of the pixels in the selected frames.
5. Write the modified frames into a new output video file.
6. Display the progress and completion status.

Steps to Extract and Decrypt Text


Open the VideoTextExtractor:


Step 1. Click Extract Text to open application.
Step 2. Select the Video with Embedded Text:
Step 3. Use the application's file dialog to select the video with the embedded text.
Set the Frame Range for Extraction:
Step 4. Enter the same frame range used for embedding text in the VideoSteganographyApp.
Extract the Text:
Step 5. Click the "Extract Text" button. The app will process the frames and extract any text present.

Decrypt the Extracted Text:
Step 6. Enter the same password used for encryption in the VideoSteganographyApp.
Step 7. Click the "Decrypt Text" button. The decrypted text should appear in the text area.

Save or Display the Text (Optional):
Step 8. You can choose to save the text to a file or display it in the application.

In cybersecurity, video steganography is one of the vital medium for covert communication, which enables the hiding of confidential and secret data within video files while preserving their visual quality. The Least Significant Bit (LSB) technique has been widely used in video steganography due to its simplicity and efficiency. However, existing LSB-based systems suffer from limitations such as low embedding capacity and vulnerability to steganalysis attacks.
The LSB techniques also known as Least Significant Bit techniques is a widely used method in steganography for hiding secret data within a digital medium, such as a video file. It subtly alters the least significant bit of each pixel in the video to embed secret data, effectively hiding it within the digital medium with minimal visual impact.

Langkah-langkah untuk Menanam & Mengenkripsi Teks Tersembunyi Menggunakan LSB


Menyediakan Teks untuk Ditanam:


Langkah 1: Buka Aplikasi
Klik "Tanam Teks" untuk membuka aplikasi.

Langkah 2: Pilih Fail Video
Klik butang "Telusuri" untuk memilih fail video yang ingin anda gunakan.
Aplikasi akan mengira jumlah keseluruhan bingkai dalam video dan memaparkannya.

Langkah 3: Pemilihan Bingkai
Masukkan nombor bingkai di mana anda ingin teks ditanam. Ini perlu ditentukan sebagai nilai yang dipisahkan dengan koma (contohnya, 1,5,10).

Langkah 4: Masukkan Teks untuk Ditanam
Taip teks yang ingin anda tanam dalam bingkai-bingkai yang dipilih dalam medan "Masukkan Teks Tersembunyi".

Langkah 5: Tetapkan Fail Video Keluaran
Berikan laluan dan nama fail untuk video keluaran yang akan mengandungi teks yang ditanam.

Langkah 6: Masukkan Kunci Pengekodan/Kata Laluan
Taip kata laluan atau kunci pengekodan yang akan digunakan untuk mengenkripsi teks.

Langkah 7: Pilih Kekuatan Pengekodan dan Mod Kiperkakan
Pilih kekuatan pengekodan yang diinginkan (AES-128 atau AES-256) dan mod kiperkakan (CBC - Pengekodan Rantaian Blok atau GCM - Mod Galois/Hitungan) dari menu terjun.

Langkah 8: Tanam dan Enkripsi Teks
Klik butang "Tanam Teks pada Bingkai". Aplikasi akan:
1. Mengenkripsi teks menggunakan kunci, kekuatan, dan mod yang ditentukan.
2. Menukar teks yang dienkripsi menjadi siri bit.
3. Melaksanakan pembetulan kesilapan terhadap bit-bit ini (menggunakan kod Reed-Solomon).
4. Menanam bit-bit ini ke dalam bit-bit kurang penting dalam piksel-piksel pada bingkai-bingkai yang dipilih.
5. Menulis bingkai-bingkai yang telah diubahsuai ke dalam fail video keluaran yang baru.
6. Memaparkan kemajuan dan status penyelesaian.
